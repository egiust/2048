How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Note: This site is NOT the official version of 2048. You might find it more pleasant to play because it is much easier to win. However, in the words of Jean-Baptiste Poquelin dit Molière :

“The greater the obstacle, the more glory in overcoming it ; and the difficulties with which we are met are the maids of honour which set off virtue.”

In short, you'd better win to the real deal if you want to brag about it and increase your self esteem. My pet monkey managed to win this game. And he has Alzheimer's.
